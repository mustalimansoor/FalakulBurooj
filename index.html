Falak al-Buruj

The Sphere of the Fixed Stars

The topic of today's waʿz mubarak was Falak al-Buruj, which in Islamic cosmological sciences is referred to as the Sphere of the Fixed Stars. This lies directly beneath al-Falak al-Muheet and contains countless stars, named for the figures, creatures, and symbols these stars appear to form.

"They speak of the stars, influencing humankind, then what of him, whose influence is upon the stars?"

Conclusion

The grief of Maulatona Zainab AS as she witnessed the events of Karbala, holding the horse steady for Imam Husain AS to mount when Maulana Abbas AS had given his arms and left him alone? It was his sister who held the horse steady for him to mount. Imam Husain AS gave his last will to Maulatona Zainab AS, and as he told her of what was to come he said, "Why are you distraught now, there are many torments yet to come! Sakina should always feel that her father is present!" As she heard these words Zainab's AS distress surged, "The Panjatan have all been slain, why is it that death will not come to Zainab!".

May Allah Taʿala grant our beloved Aqa Maula TUS, the bearer of all the beautiful qualities of the buruj, a long life in health and happiness until the Day of Qiyamat.
